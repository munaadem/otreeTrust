This game is played by pairs of individuals. Each pair is made up of a Player A and a Player B. Player A will have some information about Player B but will not be told who the players are during or after the experiment.Player B will have no information about Player A.

To start, Player A receives {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}; Player B receives nothing. Player A will then decide how much of the {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} they will send to Player B. Before Player B receives this amount, it will be multiplied by {{ C.MULTIPLICATION_FACTOR }}. Once Player B receives the tripled amount, they can then decide to send some or all of it to back A.

You will play this game 2 times with 2 different people. The more points you obtain, the more successful you will be.
